In this project, we employed AI to generate masks for our subjects, followed by the creation of customized filters to enhance the images. The task posed a challenge, given the imperfections in the AI-generated masks. Overcoming this hurdle, I successfully crafted filters that elevated the visual appeal of the images, showcasing my adaptability and problem-solving skills in AI image processing.

I designed the entire alphabet and numbers 0-9 using a mere 12 parameters. This project was a blend of technical prowess and creative ingenuity. Despite the constraints, I demonstrated my ability to achieve complex outcomes efficiently, showcasing a minimalist yet impactful approach to typographic design.
